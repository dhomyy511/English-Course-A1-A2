# 📙 Level A2: Elementary English

Welcome to Level A2 - the next step in your English journey!

## 🎯 What You'll Learn in A2

- Describing your home and neighborhood
- Talking about work and jobs
- Travel and directions
- Health and illnesses
- Hobbies and interests in more detail
- Past events and experiences

## 📚 A2 Grammar Topics

- Past Simple (regular and irregular verbs)
- Comparatives and superlatives
- 'There is / There are'
- Quantifiers (some, any, much, many, a lot of)
- Adverbs of frequency (always, usually, often)
- Present Perfect (basic use)

## 🗂️ Units in A2

1. **Unit 01: Home & Neighborhood** - Where you live
2. **Unit 02: Work & Jobs** - Career and daily work
3. **Unit 03: Travel & Directions** - Getting around
4. **Unit 04: Health & Illness** - Talking about health
5. **Unit 05: Hobbies & Interests** - What you love doing
6. **Unit 06: Review & Assessment** - A2 final review
