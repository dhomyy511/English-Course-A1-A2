## Review Topics / مواضيع المراجعة 📋

### English 🇬🇧
Time to review everything you learned in A1! Let's practice and get ready for Level A2!

We will review:

### العربية 🇸🇦
حان الوقت لمراجعة كل ما تعلمته في المستوى A1! دعنا نمارس ونستعد للمستوى A2!

سنراجع:

We will review:
- **Grammar**: Verb 'to be', Present Simple, Present Continuous, 'Going to' future, Possessives, Questions, 'Can', 'Like + -ing'
- **Vocabulary**: Greetings, Family, School, Shopping, Entertainment, Numbers, Days, Months
- **Skills**: Speaking, Listening, Reading, Writing

## 📖 Section 1: Grammar Review

### 1.1 Verb 'to be'
Fill in the blanks with 'am', 'is', or 'are':
1. I _______ from Egypt.
2. She _______ a teacher.
3. They _______ students.
4. We _______ friends.
5. He _______ 12 years old.

### 1.2 Present Simple
Fill in the correct form of the verb:
1. I _______ (study) English every day.
2. She _______ (go) to school at 8:00.
3. They _______ (play) football after school.
4. He _______ (like) math.
5. We _______ (watch) TV in the evening.

Make questions:
1. (you / like / English)? ________
2. (she / study / math)? ________

### 1.3 Present Continuous
What are they doing?
1. She _______ (listen) to music.
2. They _______ (play) football.
3. I _______ (read) a book.

### 1.4 'Going to' Future
Make sentences:
1. I / watch / a movie → _______
2. She / visit / her friend → _______
3. We / go / to the park → _______

### 1.5 Possessives
Choose the correct word:
1. This is _______ book. (my / me / I)
2. _______ father is a doctor. (His / He / Him)
3. That is _______ pen. (your / you / yours)
4. _______ house is big. (Our / We / Us)

### 1.6 Questions
Make questions:
1. (What / your name)? ________
2. (Where / you live)? ________
3. (How old / you)? ________
4. (What time / is it)? ________

### 1.7 'Can'
Complete the sentences:
1. I _______ swim. (can / can't)
2. _______ you help me? (Can / Do)
3. She _______ speak English. (can / can't)

### 1.8 'Like + -ing'
Complete the sentences:
1. I like _______ (read).
2. She likes _______ (swim).
3. They like _______ (play) football.

## 📖 Section 3: Reading Review

Read the text and answer the questions:

Hi! My name is Omar. I'm 12 years old. I'm from Cairo, Egypt. I'm a student. I go to school from Sunday to Thursday.

I have a big family. My father is a doctor. My mother is a teacher. I have one brother and one sister. My brother is 10 and my sister is 8.

In my free time, I like playing football and swimming. I don't like drawing. On weekends, I'm going to play football with my friends.

**Questions:**
1. How old is Omar?
2. Where is he from?
3. What does his father do?
4. What does he like doing?
5. What is he going to do this weekend?

## 📖 Section 4: Writing Review

Write about yourself! Use these questions:
- What's your name?
- Where are you from?
- How old are you?
- What do you like doing?
- What don't you like doing?
- What are you going to do this weekend?

**Example:**
Hello! My name is Fatima. I'm from Alexandria, Egypt. I'm 11 years old. I like reading and drawing. I don't like playing football. This weekend, I'm going to visit my grandparents.

## 📝 A1 Final Practice Test

### Part 1: Grammar (20 points)
1. I _______ from Saudi. (am / is / are)
2. She _______ a student. (am / is / are)
3. They _______ friends. (am / is / are)
4. I _______ (study) English.
5. She _______ (go) to school.
6. He _______ (like) math.
7. I like _______ (read).
8. She _______ (listen) to music now.
9. I _______ (watch) a movie tonight.
10. This is _______ book. (my / me / I)

### Part 2: Vocabulary (20 points)
Match the words:
1. Hello - a. أهلاً
2. Father - b. أخت
3. School - c. أب
4. Red - d. مدرسة
5. Reading - e. أحمر
6. Sister - f. قراءة
7. Book - g. كتاب
8. Teacher - h. مدرس
9. Football - i. كرة القدم
10. Cinema - j. سينما

### Part 3: Reading (10 points)
Read and answer:
My name is Sarah. I'm 10. I like swimming and drawing. I don't like football.

1. What's her name?
2. How old is she?
3. What does she like?
4. What doesn't she like?

### Part 4: Writing (10 points)
Write 5 sentences about yourself.
