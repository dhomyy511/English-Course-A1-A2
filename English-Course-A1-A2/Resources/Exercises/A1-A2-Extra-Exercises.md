## 📘 A1 Extra Exercises | تمارين إضافية للمستوى A1

### 1. Verb 'To Be'
Fill in 'am', 'is', or 'are':
1. I ___ a student.
2. She ___ a teacher.
3. They ___ friends.
4. We ___ from Egypt.
5. He ___ 12 years old.

### 2. Present Simple
Choose the correct form:
1. He (play / plays) football every day.
2. I (like / likes) apples.
3. She (go / goes) to school.
4. We (watch / watches) TV.
5. You (study / studies) English.

### 3. Questions
Make questions:
1. (you / like / apples)?
2. (she / go / to school)?
3. (they / play / football)?
4. (what / your name)?
5. (where / you live)?

## 📙 A2 Extra Exercises | تمارين إضافية للمستوى A2

### 1. Past Simple
Write the past form:
1. go → ___
2. eat → ___
3. do → ___
4. see → ___
5. play → ___

### 2. Comparatives & Superlatives
Fill in the blanks:
1. Cairo is (big) than Alexandria.
2. The Nile is (long) river in the world.
3. This book is (good) than that one.

### 3. Present Perfect
Make sentences:
1. (you / ever / be) to Paris?
2. I (never / try) sushi.
3. She (visit) London three times.

## 📊 Answer Key | مفتاح الإجابات

### A1 Answers
1. am, is, are, are, is
2. plays, like, goes, watch, study
3. Do you like apples? Does she go to school? Do they play football? What is your name? Where do you live?

### A2 Answers
1. went, ate, did, saw, played
2. bigger, the longest, better
3. Have you ever been, have never tried, has visited
