## 📖 Section 2: Grammar - Verb 'to be'

### What is 'to be'?
The verb 'to be' is the most important verb in English. We use it to talk about:
- Who we are (name, job)
- How we feel
- Where we are from

### Present Tense of 'to be'

| Subject | Verb | Example | العربية |
|---------|------|---------|---------|
| I | am | I am Sarah. | أنا سارة. |
| You | are | You are from Egypt. | أنت من مصر. |
| He | is | He is a teacher. | هو مدرس. |
| She | is | She is 20 years old. | هي بعمر 20 سنة. |
| It | is | It is a book. | هذا كتاب. |
| We | are | We are students. | نحن طلاب. |
| They | are | They are from Saudi. | هم من السعودية. |

### Contractions (Short Forms)
| Full | Contraction |
|------|-------------|
| I am | I'm |
| You are | You're |
| He is | He's |
| She is | She's |
| It is | It's |
| We are | We're |
| They are | They're |

### Negative Form
Add **not** after the verb:
- I am not a doctor.
- He is not from Canada.
- They are not teachers.

### Questions with 'to be'
Put the verb **before** the subject:
- Am I late?
- Are you from Morocco?
- Is she your sister?

**Wh- Questions:**
- What is your name?
- Where are you from?
- How old are you?

## 📖 Section 3: Dialogue - First Meeting

**Sarah:** Hello! I'm Sarah. What's your name?  
**Ali:** Hi Sarah! I'm Ali. Nice to meet you.  
**Sarah:** Nice to meet you too, Ali. Where are you from?  
**Ali:** I'm from Egypt. And you?  
**Sarah:** I'm from the UK. How old are you, Ali?  
**Ali:** I'm 22. Are you a student?  
**Sarah:** Yes, I am! I study English.  
**Ali:** That's great! See you later!  
**Sarah:** Goodbye, Ali!

## 📖 Section 4: Reading - My New Friend

Hi! My name is Maria. I'm from Brazil. I'm 21 years old. I'm a student at university. I study business.

This is my friend, Tom. He is from Canada. He is 23 years old. He is a teacher. He teaches math.

We are good friends! We like the same music and we like coffee!

**Comprehension Questions:**
1. Where is Maria from?
2. How old is Tom?
3. What does Tom teach?
4. Are Maria and Tom friends?

## 📖 Section 5: Writing - Introduce Yourself

### Exercise: Write about yourself

Use this template:

```
Hello! My name is _______. I'm from _______. I'm _______ years old. I am a _______.
I like _______. Nice to meet you!
```

**Example:**
Hello! My name is Sara. I'm from Lebanon. I'm 25 years old. I am a doctor.
I like reading books. Nice to meet you!
