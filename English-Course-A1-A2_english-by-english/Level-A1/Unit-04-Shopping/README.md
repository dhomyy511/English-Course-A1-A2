## 📖 Section 2: Grammar - 'This/That/These/Those'

Use these words to point to things:

| Singular | Plural | Distance | Example |
|----------|--------|----------|---------|
| This | These | Near | This is my t-shirt. / These are my shoes. |
| That | Those | Far | That is your jacket. / Those are your socks. |

**Examples:**
- This is a red shirt.
- That is a blue dress.
- These are black jeans.
- Those are white shoes.

---

## 📖 Section 3: Grammar - Shopping Language

### 'Can' for Requests
- **Can** I help you?
- **Can** I try this on?
- **Can** I have a smaller size?
- **Can** you help me, please?

### Asking About Prices
- **How much** is this?
- **How much** are these?
- It's 20 dollars.
- They're 50 dollars.

---

## 📖 Section 4: Dialogue - At the Clothes Shop

**Salesperson:** Can I help you?  
**Sarah:** Yes, please. I'm looking for a t-shirt.  
**Salesperson:** What color would you like?  
**Sarah:** Blue, please.  
**Salesperson:** What size are you?  
**Sarah:** Medium.  
**Salesperson:** Here you are. You can try it on. The fitting room is over there.  
**Sarah:** Thank you.  
(Sarah tries on the t-shirt)  
**Sarah:** It's perfect! How much is it?  
**Salesperson:** It's 15 dollars.  
**Sarah:** Great! I'll take it.  
**Salesperson:** Here you are. Thank you!

## 📖 Section 5: Reading - My Favorite Clothes

Hi! My name is Ali. I like clothes! My favorite clothes are jeans and t-shirts. I have blue jeans, black jeans, and gray jeans. I have t-shirts in many colors: red, blue, green, and black.

I wear a jacket when it's cold. I have a black jacket and a brown jacket. In summer, I wear shorts and sandals. In winter, I wear a coat, a scarf, and gloves.

Today, I'm wearing my favorite t-shirt: it's blue! And I'm wearing blue jeans and white shoes.

**Comprehension Questions:**
1. What are Ali's favorite clothes?
2. What colors are his t-shirts?
3. What does he wear when it's cold?
4. What does he wear in summer?
5. What is he wearing today?

## 📖 Section 6: Writing - Describe Your Clothes

### Exercise: Write about your favorite clothes

Use these questions:
- What are your favorite clothes?
- What colors do you like?
- What do you wear in summer?
- What do you wear in winter?
- What are you wearing today?

**Example:**
Hello! My name is Fatima. My favorite clothes are dresses and skirts. I like pink and blue. In summer, I wear dresses and sandals. In winter, I wear a coat and a scarf. Today, I'm wearing a pink dress and white shoes.
