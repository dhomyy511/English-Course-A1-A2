## 📖 Section 2: Grammar - 'Like + -ing'

Use 'Like + -ing' for things you enjoy doing:
- I **like reading**.
- She **likes swimming**.
- They **like playing football**.
- We **like watching TV**.

**Don't like + -ing:**
- I **don't like cooking**.
- He **doesn't like drawing**.

**Questions:**
- Do you **like listening to music**?
- Does she **like dancing**?

---

## 📖 Section 3: Grammar - 'Going to' Future

Use 'Going to' to talk about future plans and intentions:

**Positive:**
- I **'m going to** watch a movie tonight.
- She **'s going to** visit her friend tomorrow.
- We **'re going to** go to the park on Saturday.

**Negative:**
- I **'m not going to** stay home.
- He **isn't going to** play football.

**Questions:**
- **Are you going to** watch TV?
- **What are you going to** do this weekend?

---

## 📖 Section 4: Grammar - Present Continuous

Use Present Continuous to talk about things happening now:

**Form:** Subject + am/is/are + verb-ing

**Examples:**
- I **'m reading** a book.
- She **'s listening** to music.
- They **'re playing** football.

## 📖 Section 5: Dialogue - Weekend Plans

**Sarah:** Hi Ali! What are you doing?  
**Ali:** Hi Sarah! I'm watching TV. What about you?  
**Sarah:** I'm listening to music. What are you going to do this weekend?  
**Ali:** I'm going to play football on Saturday. And I'm going to go to the cinema on Sunday.  
**Sarah:** What movie are you going to watch?  
**Ali:** I'm going to watch a comedy. Do you like comedies?  
**Sarah:** Yes, I do! But I like action movies more. What are you doing now?  
**Ali:** I'm going to the park with my friends. Do you want to come?  
**Sarah:** Yes, please!

## 📖 Section 6: Reading - My Hobbies

Hi! My name is Fatima. I have many hobbies! I like reading books. My favorite books are stories. I also like listening to music. I like pop music. I don't like rock music.

In my free time, I like drawing. I draw animals and flowers. I also like cooking. I can make cookies and cakes.

On weekends, I like going to the park with my family. We play games and have a picnic. Sometimes, I go to the cinema with my friends. We like watching comedies.

**Comprehension Questions:**
1. What does Fatima like reading?
2. What type of music does she like?
3. What does she like drawing?
4. What does she do on weekends?
5. What type of movies does she like?

## 📖 Section 7: Writing - My Hobbies

### Exercise: Write about your hobbies

Use these questions:
- What do you like doing in your free time?
- What are your hobbies?
- What don't you like doing?
- What are you going to do this weekend?

**Example:**
Hello! My name is Omar. I like playing football and swimming. I don't like drawing. I also like watching action movies. This weekend, I'm going to play football with my friends on Saturday. And I'm going to watch a movie on Sunday.
