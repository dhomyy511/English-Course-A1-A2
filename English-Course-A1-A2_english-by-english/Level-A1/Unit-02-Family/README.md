## 📖 Section 2: Grammar - Possessives

### Possessive Adjectives
Use these before a noun:

| English | Arabic | Example |
|---------|--------|---------|
| my | لي | my father |
| your | لك | your mother |
| his | له | his brother |
| her | لها | her sister |
| its | له (غير عاقل) | its name |
| our | لنا | our family |
| their | لهم | their house |

### Possessive 's
Add **'s** to show something belongs to someone:
- **Ahmed's** book
- **Sara's** mother
- **The children's** toys

For plural nouns ending with **s**, just add **'**:
- The **boys'** room
- My **parents'** house

## 📖 Section 3: Dialogue - My Family

**Lisa:** Look at this photo! This is my family.  
**Tom:** Wow! Who is this?  
**Lisa:** This is my father. His name is John.  
**Tom:** Is he a teacher?  
**Lisa:** No, he's a doctor. And this is my mother, Mary. She's a teacher.  
**Tom:** Who are these two?  
**Lisa:** They're my brother and sister. My brother is 10 and my sister is 8.  
**Tom:** Are they students?  
**Lisa:** Yes! They go to school near our house.
