# 📘 Level A1: Beginner English

Welcome to Level A1 - the starting point for your English journey!

## 🎯 What You'll Learn in A1

- Basic greetings and introductions
- Personal information (name, age, nationality)
- Family members and relationships
- Numbers, dates, and time
- School and classroom vocabulary
- Clothes and shopping
- Hobbies and free time
- Weather and seasons

## 📚 A1 Grammar Topics

- Verb 'to be' (am, is, are)
- Present Simple
- Present Continuous
- 'Going to' future
- Articles (a, an, the)
- Possessives ('s, my, your)
- Prepositions of place (in, on, under)
- Questions (Yes/No, Wh-)

## 🗂️ Units in A1

1. **Unit 01: Greetings & Introductions** - Meeting people
2. **Unit 02: Family & Relationships** - Talking about family
3. **Unit 03: School & Daily Routine** - School life
4. **Unit 04: Shopping & Clothes** - Buying things
5. **Unit 05: Entertainment & Free Time** - Hobbies and activities
6. **Unit 06: Review & Assessment** - Practice and test
