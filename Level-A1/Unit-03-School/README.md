## 📖 Section 2: Grammar - Present Simple

We use Present Simple for:
1. Habits and routines
2. Things we do regularly
3. Facts and general truths

### Positive Sentences
| Subject | Verb | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| I / You / We / They | base verb | I study English. |
| He / She / It | verb + s | She studies math. |

**Spelling Rules for He/She/It:**
- Add **s**: work → works, play → plays
- Add **es** (ch, sh, s, x, o): watch → watches, go → goes
- **y** → **ies** (consonant + y): study → studies, fly → flies

### Negative Sentences
Use **don't** or **doesn't**:
- I **don't** like math.
- You **don't** go to school on Friday.
- He **doesn't** study science.
- She **doesn't** watch TV in the morning.

### Questions
Use **do** or **does**:
- **Do** you like school?
- **Do** they play football?
- **Does** he study English?
- **Does** she watch TV?

**Wh- Questions:**
- What **do** you study?
- When **do** you go to school?
- Where **does** he live?

## 📖 Section 4: Dialogue - School Routine

**Sarah:** Hi Ali! What time do you wake up?  
**Ali:** I wake up at seven o'clock.  
**Sarah:** What time do you go to school?  
**Ali:** I go to school at eight.  
**Sarah:** What subjects do you study?  
**Ali:** I study math, English, and science.  
**Sarah:** Do you like math?  
**Ali:** Yes, I do! But I don't like history.  
**Sarah:** What time do you finish school?  
**Ali:** I finish school at two o'clock in the afternoon.  
**Sarah:** What do you do after school?  
**Ali:** I do my homework and then I watch TV.

## 📖 Section 5: Reading - My School Day

Hi! My name is Omar. I'm 12 years old. I go to school from Sunday to Thursday.

I wake up at 7:00. I eat breakfast at 7:30. I usually eat bread and milk. Then I go to school at 8:00.

School starts at 8:30. I have 6 lessons a day. My favorite subject is English because it's interesting. I don't like math because it's difficult.

I eat lunch at school at 12:30. School finishes at 2:00. I go home and do my homework. Then I play football with my friends. I eat dinner at 7:00. I go to bed at 9:00.

**Comprehension Questions:**
1. How old is Omar?
2. What time does he wake up?
3. What is his favorite subject?
4. What doesn't he like?
5. What time does he go to bed?

## 📖 Section 6: Writing - My Daily Routine

### Exercise: Write about your daily routine

Use these questions to help you:
- What time do you wake up?
- What do you eat for breakfast?
- What time do you go to school?
- What is your favorite subject?
- What time do you finish school?
- What do you do after school?
- What time do you go to bed?

**Example:**
Hello! My name is Fatima. I wake up at 7:30. I eat eggs and bread for breakfast. I go to school at 8:30. My favorite subject is art. I finish school at 2:00. After school, I do my homework and read a book. I eat dinner at 7:30. I go to bed at 9:30.
