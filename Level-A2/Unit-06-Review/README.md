## 📖 Section 1: Grammar Review

### 1.1 Past Simple
Complete with the correct form:
1. I (visit) Alexandria last year.
2. She (not go) to the party.
3. (they / eat) lunch?
4. We (play) football yesterday.

### 1.2 Present Perfect
Make sentences:
1. (you / ever / be) to Cairo?
2. I (never / try) sushi.
3. She (visit) Paris three times.
4. (he / finish) his homework?

### 1.3 Comparatives & Superlatives
Fill in the blanks:
1. Cairo is (big) than Alexandria.
2. The Nile is (long) river in the world.
3. This book is (interesting) than that one.
4. This is (good) restaurant in town.

### 1.4 Should/Shouldn't
Give advice:
1. You (should / shouldn't) smoke.
2. You (should / shouldn't) exercise every day.
3. You (should / shouldn't) drink lots of water.

### 1.5 Too/Enough
Fill in 'too' or 'enough':
1. The coffee is (too / enough) hot to drink.
2. I'm not old (too / enough) to drive.
3. Is there (too / enough) food?

### 1.6 Prepositions of Time
Fill in 'in', 'on', or 'at':
1. I was born (in / on / at) 2005.
2. We're going (in / on / at) July.
3. The party is (in / on / at) Friday.
4. I get up (in / on / at) 7 AM.

## 📖 Section 3: Reading Review

Read the text and answer the questions:

Hi! My name is Ali. Last summer, I went to Hurghada with my family. We traveled by car. It took about 4 hours. We stayed in a hotel near the beach. The hotel was the best one I've ever stayed in!

We went swimming every day. I've never seen such beautiful water! We also went diving. It was amazing! I tried many new foods. The fish was the most delicious I've ever eaten!

I've been to many places, but Hurghada is my favorite. I want to go back again next year!

**Questions**:
1. Where did Ali go last summer?
2. How did he travel there?
3. Where did he stay?
4. What did he do there?
5. Does he want to go back?

## 📝 A2 Final Practice Test

### Part 1: Grammar (20 points)
1. I (visit) my grandmother yesterday.
2. She (never / be) to Paris.
3. This book is (interesting) than that one.
4. You (should / shouldn't) eat too much sugar.
5. The bag is (too / enough) heavy.
6. I was born (in / on / at) 1995.
7. (there / be) a park near here?
8. She (play) tennis every weekend.
9. I (read) a great book last week.
10. (you / ever / try) Indian food?

### Part 2: Vocabulary (20 points)
Match the words:
1. Kitchen - a. طبيب
2. Doctor - b. مطبخ
3. Plane - c. طائرة
4. Headache - d. صداع
5. Reading - e. قراءة

### Part 3: Reading (10 points)
Read and answer:
Last weekend, Sarah went to the park. She played football with her friends. She had a great time!
1. Where did Sarah go?
2. What did she do?
3. Did she have a good time?
