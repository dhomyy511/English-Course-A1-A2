## 📖 Section 5: Dialogue - Talking about Hobbies

**Sarah:** Hi Ali! What do you like doing in your free time?  
**Ali:** Hi Sarah! I love playing football and basketball. What about you?  
**Sarah:** I enjoy reading and painting. Have you ever tried painting?  
**Ali:** No, I haven't! I'm not very creative. Do you prefer reading or writing?  
**Sarah:** I prefer reading. I think reading is more interesting than writing!  
**Ali:** What's your favorite book?  
**Sarah:** My favorite book is "Harry Potter". It's amazing!  
**Ali:** Cool! I've never read it. Maybe I should try!

## 📖 Section 6: Reading - My Hobby

Hi! My name is Fatima. My favorite hobby is photography! I've loved taking photos since I was 10 years old. My dad gave me my first camera. I like taking photos of nature, animals, and people.

I've taken photos in many places - in Cairo, Alexandria, and even in the desert! The best photo I've ever taken is of the pyramids at sunset. It's fantastic!

I also enjoy sharing my photos online. I think photography is a great hobby because it lets you see the world in a different way. Have you ever tried photography?

**Comprehension Questions:**
1. What is Fatima's favorite hobby?
2. When did she start taking photos?
3. What does she like taking photos of?
4. Where has she taken photos?
5. What's the best photo she's ever taken?

## 📖 Section 7: Writing - Your Hobby

### Exercise: Write about your favorite hobby

Answer these questions:
- What is your favorite hobby?
- When did you start?
- What do you like about it?
- Have you ever done it with friends?
- Would you like to try something new?

**Example:**
Hi! My name is Omar. My favorite hobby is playing football! I started playing when I was 8 years old. I love it because it's fun and it keeps me healthy. I play with my friends every weekend. We've played in many matches. I've never played for a real team, but I'd like to try one day!

## 📖 Section 9: Speaking - Talk about Hobbies

### Role Play: Discuss Hobbies
**Person A:** What do you like doing in your free time?  
**Person B:** I like _______. What about you?  
**Person A:** I love _______. Have you ever tried _______?  
**Person B:** Yes, I have! / No, I haven't. Do you prefer _______ or _______?  
**Person A:** I prefer _______. It's more _______!

### Useful Phrases
- What's your hobby?
- I like / love / enjoy...
- I prefer... to...
- I'd rather... than...
- Have you ever...?
- I've never...
- It's interesting / exciting / boring...

## 📝 Unit Exercises

### Exercise 1: Adjectives of Opinion
Match the adjectives:
1. Interesting - a. ممل
2. Boring - b. ممتع
3. Exciting - c. رائع
4. Fantastic - d. مثير

### Exercise 2: Preferences

1. I prefer reading (to / than) writing.
2. I'd rather play football (to / than) basketball.
3. My favorite sport (is / are) tennis.
