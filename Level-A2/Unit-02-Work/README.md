## 📖 Section 2: Grammar - Adverbs of Frequency

Use these adverbs to talk about how often you do things:

| Adverb | Meaning | Frequency |
|--------|---------|-----------|
| always | دائماً | 100% |
| usually | عادةً | 80% |
| often | غالباً | 60% |
| sometimes | أحياناً | 40% |
| rarely | نادراً | 20% |
| never | أبداً | 0% |

**Position**: Before the main verb, after 'be':
- I **always** go to school at 8.
- She **is usually** tired in the evening.
- They **sometimes** play football.

## 📖 Section 3: Grammar - Past Simple (Regular Verbs)

Use Past Simple to talk about completed actions in the past.

### Regular Verbs: Add -ed
- work → worked
- play → played
- live → lived
- stop → stopped
- study → studied

**Spelling Rules**:
- Add -ed: work → worked
- Add -d (ends with -e): live → lived
- Double consonant (short vowel + consonant): stop → stopped
- y → ied (consonant + y): study → studied

### Positive
- I worked yesterday.
- She played football last week.

### Negative
- Use didn't + base verb:
  - I didn't work yesterday.
  - She didn't play football.

### Questions
- Use Did + subject + base verb:
  - Did you work yesterday?
  - Did she play football?

## 📖 Section 5: Dialogue - Talking About Work

**Sarah:** Hi Ali! What does your father do?  
**Ali:** Hi Sarah! He's a doctor. He works in a big hospital.  
**Sarah:** That's interesting! What time does he start work?  
**Ali:** He usually starts at 8 AM. He always gets up early.  
**Sarah:** Did he work yesterday?  
**Ali:** Yes, he did. But he didn't work on Friday.  
**Sarah:** What about your mother?  
**Ali:** She's a teacher. She works at my school! She taught me English last year.  
**Sarah:** Wow! That's great. What do you want to be in the future?  
**Ali:** I want to be an engineer!

## 📖 Section 6: Reading - My First Job

Hi! My name is Fatima. When I was 16, I got my first job. I worked in a small café in Alexandria. I worked there every weekend. I usually started at 9 AM and finished at 5 PM. I served food and drinks to customers. I sometimes made coffee too. The job was hard, but it was fun! I met a lot of nice people. The manager was very kind. I worked there for one year. Now I'm a student at university, but I sometimes remember my first job!

**Comprehension Questions:**
1. How old was Fatima when she got her first job?
2. Where did she work?
3. What did she do there?
4. Did she like the job?
5. How long did she work there?

## 📖 Section 7: Writing - Your Work Experience

### Exercise: Write about a job or your daily routine

Choose one topic:
1. Your daily routine (what you do every day, using adverbs of frequency)
2. A past experience (something you did last week/last year, using Past Simple)
3. Your dream job (what you want to be in the future)

**Example (Daily Routine):**
Hello! My name is Omar. I'm a student. I always get up at 7 AM. I usually have breakfast at 7:30. I often walk to school. I never watch TV in the morning. I sometimes play football with my friends in the afternoon.

**Example (Past Experience):**
Last summer, I went to the beach with my family. We stayed there for a week. We swam in the sea and played on the sand. The weather was great! We had a lot of fun.

### Role Play: Job Interview

**Candidate:** My name is _______.  
**Interviewer:** What do you do?  
**Candidate:** I'm a _______.  
**Interviewer:** Where do you work?

**Interviewer:** What time do you start work?

**Interviewer:** Do you like your job?
